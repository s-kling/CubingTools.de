Guildford Optimizer

This is the 2-Person Guildford Optimizer. To start, input your global times for each event in seconds for your partner and yourself.

If you aren't sure what your globals are, you can also input your WCA ID. This will return your latest Ao25 for each event! If you have more solves that you would like to be taken into account for your global, modify the Solve count to process input field.

The events in the middle are the events that both competitors could do, should they finish earlier than expected, or one competitor makes a mistake. These events should probably also be done last.

If you cannot solve a puzzle, or simply do not want to, you can input DNF as your time.
