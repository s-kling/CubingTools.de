WCA Average Calculator

This tool helps cubing enthusiasts track solve times, calculate averages, and monitor personal ranks (PR) for various events.

Input your solve times to see real-time results and compare them to your historical data. After five solves, the tool calculates and saves the average as a "tag" for future reference.
