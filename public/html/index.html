Solve Smarter, Not Harder

Welcome to CubingTools.de, your ultimate destination for enhancing your cubing performance. Our platform offers a variety of tools designed to help you achieve your best results in competitive cubing.

Groupifier

The Groupifier tool is designed to streamline the process of organizing competitors into groups for cubing competitions. It ensures that each group is balanced in terms of skill level and number of participants, making the competition fair and efficient. By using advanced algorithms, the Groupifier minimizes waiting times and maximizes the use of available resources, providing a seamless experience for both organizers and competitors.

Guildford Optimizer

The Guildford Optimizer is a powerful tool that helps you plan and optimize your events for the Guildford Challenge. It takes into account various factors such as event duration, competitor availability, and resource allocation to create the most efficient schedule possible. This tool ensures that you can complete all events within the given time frame while maximizing the performance of each competitor.

WCA Average Calculator

The WCA Average Calculator tool allows you to record and analyze your solve times with ease. It provides detailed statistics based on previous competitions to show you what PR rank a specific solve is, what time is required for the target average and more.

Global Calculator

The Global Calculator tool allows you to calculate and compare your global rankings across different events. It provides detailed insights into your performance on a global scale, helping you understand where you stand among other competitors worldwide. You can use your WCA ID to check your official global rankings, and also upload your csTimer file to see your global performance based on your practice sessions. This tool is essential for tracking your progress and setting new goals.
